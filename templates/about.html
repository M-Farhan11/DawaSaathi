About DawaSaathi

Pakistan's AI Medicine Assistant

پاکستان کا پہلا اے آئی دوا معاون

DawaSaathi was built to bridge a critical gap in healthcare accessibility across Pakistan — giving every citizen, regardless of literacy or language, the ability to understand their medicine.

The Problem

مسئلہ

40%

of Pakistanis are illiterate

پاکستانی آبادی ناخواندہ ہے

انگریزی لیبل نہیں پڑھ سکتے

240M

people who need this solution

افراد جنہیں اس کی ضرورت ہے

Incorrect dosage, missed timing, dangerous drug combinations, and unknown side effects are silent threats that affect millions of Pakistani households daily. Rural communities, the elderly, and those without access to qualified pharmacists are the most vulnerable.

Our Solution

ہمارا حل

translate

Fully Bilingual

Every piece of information is delivered in both English and Urdu (Nastaliq script), making it accessible to all literacy levels.

mic

Voice-First Design

Users who cannot read can speak the medicine name and hear the results — removing literacy as a barrier entirely.

auto_awesome

AI-Powered Accuracy

Powered by large language models to generate reliable, structured, and up-to-date medicine information on demand.

psychology

Symptom-Based Search

Don't know the medicine name? Describe your symptoms and get a safe over-the-counter recommendation with severity-aware guidance.
